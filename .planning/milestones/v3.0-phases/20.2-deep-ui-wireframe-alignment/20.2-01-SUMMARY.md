# Phase 20.2 Plan 01: Buttons & Cards Wireframe Alignment Summary

**Gradient backgrounds with inner highlights and colored shadows on primary/success/danger buttons, plus uppercase 13px card titles and hover lift transitions on cards — full wireframe v3.19.2 alignment for the two most visually impactful component families.**

## Performance

- **Duration:** ~5 min
- **Started:** 2026-03-17T10:24:10Z
- **Completed:** 2026-03-17T10:24:31Z
- **Tasks:** 2
- **Files modified:** 1

## Accomplishments

- `.btn-primary`, `.btn-success`, `.btn-danger` all have gradient backgrounds with inner white highlight (`inset 0 1px 0 rgba(255,255,255,.12)`) and downward colored shadows
- All three colored button variants lift on hover (`translateY(-1px)`) with enhanced shadow and press on active (`scale(.98) translateY(0)`)
- `.btn` base updated: `font-weight: 700`, `min-height: 40px`, visible `border: 1px solid var(--color-border)`, `transform` added to transition list
- `.card` gets smooth transition on `box-shadow`, `border-color`, `transform`, and `background` (0.18s ease)
- `.card:hover` elevates shadow to `var(--shadow-md)`, `.card-clickable:hover` adds `translateY(-2px)` lift with accent border tint
- `.card-title` redesigned to 13px, uppercase, 700 weight, 0.7px letter-spacing — affects all 15+ pages using card components

## Task Commits

1. **Task 1: Update button styles — gradient, shadow, hover lift, active press** - `0d17dec` (feat)
2. **Task 2: Update card styles — transition, hover, clickable, card-title uppercase** - `4863070` (feat)

## Files Created/Modified

- `public/assets/css/design-system.css` — Button variants (lines 1101–1165) and card components (lines 1255–1299) updated to wireframe v3.19.2 spec

## Decisions Made

- Ghost, link, and secondary buttons intentionally excluded from gradient/lift — they stay flat per wireframe specification
- `.card:hover` adds shadow elevation only (no transform lift); `.card-clickable:hover` adds the lift — keeps non-interactive cards subtle while interactive ones feel responsive
- Active press uses `scale(.98) translateY(0)` rather than just `scale(.98)` to prevent visual jump when transitioning from the hover `translateY(-1px)` state
