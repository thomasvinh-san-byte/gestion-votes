What was built:

**Members (DATA-03):** Elevated stats bar with icons and ag-tooltip on each label, 40px circular avatars with success ring on active members, hover-reveal 32px action buttons with ag-tooltip, filter chip count badges that update after filtering, polished upload zone with primary-color icon and subtle background.

**Archives (DATA-02):** Design-system KPI cards (local archives.css overrides removed), KPI icons and ag-tooltip labels, duplicate .archive-stats section removed, status filter pills (Validee/Archivee/PV envoye), hover-reveal card actions, status-colored left border accents (green/blue/orange), session-card quality hover (shadow-md + border-strong), mono dates, filter reset button on empty state.
